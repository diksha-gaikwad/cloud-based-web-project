About Cloud Models

Cloud computing offers different service models for flexibility, scalability, and control:

  • IaaS (Infrastructure as a Service): Virtual servers, storage, and networking resources.
  • PaaS (Platform as a Service): Development and deployment platforms with built-in tools.
  • SaaS (Software as a Service): Fully managed software delivered over the internet.
